# 🎬 UGC Video Maker — Free AI Tool

> Turn any product photo into a UGC-style video in 60 seconds. No coding, no server, 100% free.

**Just one HTML file. Open it. Use it.**

---

## ✅ How to Use

### Step 1 — Get a Free API Key (takes 2 min)
1. Go to [replicate.com](https://replicate.com) and create a free account
2. Visit [replicate.com/account/api-tokens](https://replicate.com/account/api-tokens)
3. Click **"Create token"** and copy it

### Step 2 — Open the Tool
1. Download `ugc-video-maker.html`
2. Double-click to open in your browser (Chrome or Edge recommended)
3. Paste your API key when prompted — it's saved locally on your computer

### Step 3 — Generate
1. Upload your product image
2. Describe your audience
3. Choose platform + aspect ratio
4. Click **Generate** — done in ~60 seconds!

---

## 💰 Is It Really Free?

Yes. Replicate gives you free credits when you sign up. Each video costs roughly **$0.05–0.10** from your credits. New accounts get enough free credits to generate many videos.
